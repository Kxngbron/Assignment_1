Services


Massages

Our signature spa massage service offers a tranquil sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation. A skilled therapist tailors the massage to individual needs, releasing tension and restoring balance. The massage begins with warm oil, followed by long strokes, kneading, and targeted pressure. The therapist ensures comfort and lingers in the relaxation area, offering additional treatments or a sense of rejuvenation. Experience the transformative power of touch at our serene spa oasis. Massages available are:
  • Deep Tissue Massage
  • Sports Massage
  • Soft Stone Massage
Facials

Our spa facial service offers a luxurious experience for skin and spirit rejuvenation. The treatment begins with a consultation to assess your skin type and concerns. A facial massage is followed by a targeted mask infused with antioxidants, vitamins, and botanical extracts. A scalp or hand massage is also provided. A lightweight moisturizer and SPF are applied to protect and nourish the newly rejuvenated skin. The experience is designed to leave you feeling refreshed and radiant, enhancing your skincare routine and promoting inner peace. Facials services available are:
  • Classic Facial
  • Chemical Peel Facial
  • Microdermabrasion
Nail Treatments

Our spa manicure and pedicure services provide a luxurious experience for your hands, feet, and senses. Our skilled nail technicians will guide you through a personalized consultation, followed by a luxurious soak, footbath, and nail polish application. The spa sanctuary offers a serene atmosphere, allowing you to relax and experience the transformative power of beauty and relaxation. Nail Treatment services available:
  • Manicure
  • Pedicure
  • Nail Refresh
